Hearing Gene Prioritization

An integrative multi-omics framework to prioritize gene therapy targets for sensorineural hearing loss.

Explore Results Read Paper (Preprint)

What is TPS?

The Therapeutic Priority Score (TPS) is a data-driven metric designed to rank genes based on their potential as therapeutic targets. By integrating single-cell transcriptomics from human and mouse inner ears across development, aging, and injury models, TPS identifies candidates that are not only essential for hearing but also amenable to intervention.
