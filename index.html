Rationale: Essential for hair cell survival and maturation. Ensures long-term viability in regenerative therapy.

SOX2 #2

Rationale: Critical regulator of supporting cell plasticity. Primes cells for transdifferentiation.

Rationale: The classic master regulator. Ranked below survival factors (POU4F3) to reflect modern combinatorial consensus.
